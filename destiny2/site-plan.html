Raids

(This page will have another link tree to all the different raids in the game.) (also I couldn't find images for some of raids but since im not including them anyway I decided not to go through the trouble.)
These 6-man activities are the magnum opus of Destiny 2. They take the mechanics you learned in other PvE content to the next level. Most encounters require a minimum of 6 people to comlpete, with optional challenges to make it that much harder. However, the weapons and armor available within these raids are top-tier. And with the introduction of weapon crafting, the hunt for the best perk rolls is much less painful. This guide will show you how to do every encounter, every challenge, find every seceret chest, and earn as many red-box weapons as possible.
Click on any of the images or links to view guides for that raid.

Images for the Page 2

Classes

(same as the raids page, just another link tree leading to the different classes in the game.)
Destiny 2 has three different classes you can play: hunter, titan, and warlock. Each of these classes have four different subclasses you can use: void, solar, arc, and stasis. All of these options play differently and there are many different ways you can build-craft within each subclass. There is a lot to cover so I will let you get into it.
Click on the class symbols below to learn more about each class and their sub-classes.
