Destiny 2 Guide

Destiny 2 is a game in dire need of a better tutorial system. New and returning players are almost completely on their own when (re)learning the game. So, while Bungie is busy not making a new PvP map, I will be here to help you on your journey into the amazing world of Destiny 2. The first page you might want to check out is the Class Guide. Each class will drastically change the way you navigate the game (literally).

Class Guide

Titan, Hunter, and Warlock are the three classes you can choose from. Each class has four unique sub-classes: Arc, Void, Solar, and Stasis.

Raid Guide

Raids are six-man, multi-encounter activities. They contain a wide variety of unique puzzle mechanics. Most players consider them to be the end-game of Destiny.

Vanguard Guide

The Vanguard playlist consists of Vanguard Ops and Nightfalls. Vanguard Ops are easy and repeatable story missions. Nightfalls add an extra layer of difficulty to Vanguard Ops.

Gambit Guide

Gambit is Bungie's most unique activity. It involves both PvE and PvP in a 4v4 race to summon and destroy a boss. Invade, gather and deposit motes, and destroy the Primeval.

Crucible Guide

The Crucible is Destiny's PvP arena. Face off against other guardians in 13 unique game modes. This is where you can really show of your first-person shooter skills.
